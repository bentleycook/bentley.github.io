What's a LiPo?

Lithium-ion polymer battery

Series (The S)

Each cell has:

  • Max voltage: 4.2v
  • Nominal voltage: 3.7v
  • Min. voltage: 3.0v
  • 2S (2 cells in series) = 7.4 volts
  • 3S (3 cells in series) = 11.1 volts

Current Rating (The C)

The maximum average recommended discharge current for the cell.

  • 2600mAh x 55C = 143 amps

Charging

Charging Scenario

Battery: 2600mAh, 3S, 11.1V, 3C

  • 1C (rec) * 2.6Ah = 2.6A charge current
  • 3S * 3.7V = 11.1V charge voltage
  • 2.6A * 12.6V (fully charged voltage) = 32.76W power draw

Fire Extinguishers

Remember

  • Never above: 4.2v
  • Store at: 3.7v
  • Never below: 3.0v
